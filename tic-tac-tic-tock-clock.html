Meet the Tic Tac Tic Toc Clock. We created this prototype desk clock to help keep things fresh through the day. It releases a Tic Tac every hour at the “Tac” position. Over 10,000 fans on Myspace voted for the clock to be made on our purpose-built Tic Tac Tic Tock Clock Myspace app. But after the change to Myspace 2.0 the page has been scrapped and funding for the clock pulled. Bummer.
