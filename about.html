ABOUT

WORK

I currently live and work in Chicago, IL..

 

I grew up in Cape Town, South Africa and have since worked at ad agencies in Dubai, Cape Town, Johannesburg, London, Paris and Chicago. I love traveling and have ventured through parts of South East Asia, Australia, India, various Island countries, Southern Africa, Europe, South and North America.

 

I'm an uber Nerd. Creative Director. Maker. Tinkerer. Art Director. Writer.

 

I have a love of the interactive, photography, design and anything that involves grease or ones and zeros.

 

Aside from advertising, I love making things. I like to build bikes: mountain, cyclocross and road, they all fascinate me and I've been messing around with them for years. They're a great excuse for me to get my hands dirty and get out and breathe some beautiful fresh air.

When the weather turns bad, you'll find my head stuck in a computer device that I'm building or tearing apart and tweaking, overclocking, or tearing the software apart. I've built hackintoshes, servers and kick-ass workstations. (If any of that makes sense to you, well done.)

 

I love more than anything, starting with the nuts and bolts and sweating and crying and watching, when suddenly, in an instant, it all works, just like it should.

 

Get in touch with me by email.
