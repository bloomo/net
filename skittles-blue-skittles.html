These were the posters we released to tease the design of the new sweet.

During 2010 we noticed that Skittles fans on Facebook had made an astute observation:  Why does the Rainbow on the pack have the colour blue in it, but there is no Blue Skittle in the pack? We got to work creating a blue Skittle and then offered it to our fans. On one condition- you'd have to like the Rainbow and rub his belly.
